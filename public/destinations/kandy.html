Kandy, Sri Lanka

Kandy is a sacred city in Sri Lanka, located in the Central Highlands. It is the last capital of the ancient kings of Sri Lanka and is home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic, one of the most sacred Buddhist sites in the world. Kandy is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is a popular tourist destination.

Here's a guide to everything you need to know about Kandy:

Places to Visit

Temple of the Tooth Relic

The Temple of the Tooth Relic is a Buddhist temple that houses the sacred tooth relic of the Buddha. It is one of the most sacred Buddhist sites in the world and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The temple is located in the heart of Kandy and is a popular tourist destination.

Kandy Lake

Kandy Lake is a large artificial lake in the center of Kandy. It was built by King Vimaladharmasuriya I in the 16th century and is a popular spot for swimming, boating, and fishing. The lake is also home to a variety of birds and animals.

Royal Botanical Garden

The Royal Botanical Garden is a 150-acre botanical garden that is home to a variety of plants and flowers. It was founded in 1821 by Sir William John Jackson and is one of the oldest botanical gardens in Asia. The garden is a popular spot for tourists and locals alike and is a great place to learn about Sri Lanka's plant life.

National Museum of Kandy

The National Museum of Kandy is a museum that houses a collection of artifacts from Sri Lanka's history. It was founded in 1877 and is located in the former palace of King Sri Vikrama Rajasinha. The museum houses a collection of artifacts from Sri Lanka's prehistory, early history, and medieval period.

Tea Plantations

Tea plantations are plantations where you can learn about and taste Sri Lanka's tea. There are a number of tea plantations located in and around Kandy. These plantations offer visitors the opportunity to learn about the different types of tea that are grown in Sri Lanka and to taste them.

Bahiravokanda Vihara

Bahiravokanda Vihara is a Buddhist temple that is home to a large statue of the Buddha. It is located on a hill overlooking Kandy and is a popular spot for pilgrims and tourists. The temple was built in the 18th century and is dedicated to the Buddha.

Udawattakele Sanctuary

Udawatta Kele Sanctuary is a forest reserve that is home to a variety of animals and plants. It is located on the outskirts of Kandy and is a popular spot for hiking, bird watching, and picnicking. The sanctuary is home to a variety of animals, including elephants, monkeys, deer, and birds.

Spice Gardens

Spice gardens are gardens where you can learn about and sample Sri Lanka's spices. There are a number of spice gardens located in and around Kandy. These gardens offer visitors the opportunity to learn about the different spices that are grown in Sri Lanka and to sample them.

Gem Museums

Gem museums are museums where you can learn about and buy Sri Lanka's gems. There are a number of gem museums located in and around Kandy. These museums offer visitors the opportunity to learn about the different gems that are found in Sri Lanka and to buy them.

Activities

Immerse Yourself in Cultural Delights

Kandy Esala Perahera

If you visit during the Esala Perahera festival (held in July/August), you can witness a grand procession featuring beautifully adorned elephants, drummers, dancers, and fire performers.

Kandy City Tour

Take a guided tour of Kandy city to explore its rich history and culture. Visit landmarks like the Kandy Lake, Kandy View Point, and historical buildings.

Kandy Traditional Dance Show

Attend a cultural dance performance showcasing traditional Kandyan dances, drumming, and fire acts. Immerse yourself in the vibrant rhythms and colorful costumes.

Spice Garden Visit

Explore a spice garden to learn about Sri Lanka's rich spice heritage. Discover aromatic spices, herbs, and medicinal plants, and gain insights into their culinary and healing properties.

Tea Plantation Tour

Embark on a tea plantation tour to learn about the tea-making process, from cultivation to production. Enjoy scenic views of lush green tea estates and savor a cup of world-renowned Ceylon tea.

Shopping

Discover Treasures in Kandy's Markets

Central Market Shopping

Explore the vibrant Central Market in Kandy, where you can immerse yourself in a bustling atmosphere. Discover a wide array of fresh produce, spices, textiles, handicrafts, and local products. Engage with the friendly vendors and experience the authentic local market culture.

Boutique Shopping in Kandy City Center

Indulge in a shopping spree at Kandy City Center, a modern shopping mall offering a range of boutique stores. Discover trendy fashion boutiques, jewelry shops, footwear stores, and accessory outlets. Find unique and stylish items to add to your wardrobe or for gifting.

Traditional Handicraft Shopping at Laksala

Visit Laksala, a government-owned handicraft emporium, to explore a rich collection of traditional Sri Lankan handicrafts. Browse through intricately carved wooden masks, batik clothing, handloom textiles, pottery, jewelry, and other artisanal crafts.

Gemstone Shopping at Ratnapura Gem Market

Embark on a gemstone shopping adventure by visiting the Ratnapura Gem Market, located near Kandy. Explore a wide range of precious and semi-precious gemstones, including sapphires, rubies, emeralds, and more. Engage with knowledgeable gem traders and find exquisite pieces to add to your jewelry collection.
